Khushipath Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Khushipath ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information and sensitive personal data collected through our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our diagnostic services, fulfill your appointments, and communicate with you. The types of data collected include:

1.1 Personal Identification Information (PII)

  • **Contact Data:** Name, email address, phone number, and residential address (for home collection services).
  • **Demographic Data:** Age, gender, and date of birth.
  • **Account Data:** Username, hashed password, and role (User/Admin).
  • **Financial Data:** Transaction details related to payments, though we do not store full credit/debit card details.

1.2 Sensitive Personal Health Information (SPHI)

  • **Diagnostic Data:** Details of tests booked (e.g., CBC, Lipid Profile), sample collection status, and laboratory test reports.
  • **Appointment Data:** Date, time, and location of sample collection appointments.

1.3 Technical and Usage Data

  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our website (e.g., pages viewed, time spent, packages browsed).
  • **Device Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following primary purposes:

  • To **Process and Fulfill Orders:** To schedule appointments, conduct home sample collection, and provide test results.
  • To **Authenticate and Manage Your Account:** To verify identity, allow login, and manage user roles.
  • For **Communication:** To send appointment confirmations, result notifications, and respond to your queries (via email or the Contact Form).
  • For **Internal Operations:** To improve service quality, troubleshoot technical issues, and analyze usage trends.
  • For **Legal Compliance:** To meet regulatory requirements related to healthcare and data privacy.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • **With Lab Partners:** Health data (SPHI) is shared with accredited partner laboratories solely for the purpose of processing your requested tests.
  • **With Payment Gateways:** Financial data is shared securely with third-party payment processors to complete transactions.
  • **For Legal Reasons:** When required by law, court order, or government authority to prevent fraud or protect the safety and rights of Khushipath or its users.

4. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • **Encryption:** Sensitive data, including passwords (hashed) and transmission of health reports, is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols (e.g., HTTPS).
  • **Access Control:** Access to SPHI is strictly limited to authorized personnel who require it to perform their duties (e.g., lab technicians, appointment managers).
  • **Secure Storage:** Data is stored in secured databases (MongoDB) with access firewalls and regular security audits.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal data, subject to local laws:

  • **Access:** You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • **Correction:** You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data (e.g., updating your address or email).
  • **Deletion:** You can request the deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention obligations for medical records.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
