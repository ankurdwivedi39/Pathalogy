Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 8-10 hours of overnight fasting is preferred (due to the added context markers like Glucose), though not mandatory for the thyroid test itself.
  • **Thyroid Medication:** If taking medication, the sample should be collected **before** taking your morning dose.
  • **Supplements:** Avoid Biotin, Vitamin D, and B12 supplements for 48 hours prior for accurate baseline results.
