Iron Profile

Measures different components of iron status: circulating iron, iron-binding capacity (TIBC), and transferrin saturation. Essential for diagnosing and differentiating types of anemia.

What It Measures

  • **Serum Iron:** Measures the amount of iron circulating in the blood.
  • **Total Iron Binding Capacity (TIBC):** Measures the capacity of transferrin to bind iron.
  • **Transferrin Saturation:** Calculates the percentage of transferrin that is saturated with iron.
  • Helps distinguish between iron deficiency anemia and chronic disease anemia.

Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** A **MANDATORY 10-12 hour fast** is required. Iron levels fluctuate, and fasting ensures the most accurate reading.
  • **Supplements:** Avoid taking iron supplements for **24 hours** prior to the test.
  • **Timing:** Ideally, the test should be performed in the morning (before 10 AM) as iron levels naturally drop throughout the day.
