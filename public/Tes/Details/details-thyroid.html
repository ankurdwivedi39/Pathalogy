What It Measures

  • **TSH** (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone): Primary indicator of thyroid function.
  • **T3 (Total):** Measures triiodothyronine hormone.
  • **T4 (Total):** Measures thyroxine hormone.
  • Crucial for diagnosing hypo- or hyperthyroidism.

Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** **Not required** for this test.
  • **Medication:** If you take thyroid medication, collect the sample **before** taking your morning dose.
