What It Measures

  • **Sodium (Na+):** Essential for maintaining fluid balance (hydration).
  • **Potassium (K+):** Critical for nerve, muscle, and heart function.
  • **Chloride (Cl-):** Helps maintain proper fluid levels and acid-base balance.
  • Used for monitoring patients with diarrhea, vomiting, heart failure, and kidney disease.

Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** **Not required** for this test.
  • **Hydration:** Remain adequately hydrated prior to collection.
  • **Medication:** Inform your phlebotomist about any diuretics (water pills) or blood pressure medications you are taking, as they significantly affect these readings.
