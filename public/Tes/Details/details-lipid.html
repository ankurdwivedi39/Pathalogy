What It Measures

  • **Total Cholesterol & Triglycerides:** Measures overall fat levels in the blood.
  • **HDL Cholesterol:** High-Density Lipoprotein, known as "Good Cholesterol."
  • **LDL Cholesterol:** Low-Density Lipoprotein, known as "Bad Cholesterol."
  • Crucial for diagnosing dyslipidemia and heart disease risk.

Preparation Guidelines (Fasting is Critical)

  • **Fasting:** A **MANDATORY 10-12 hour fast** is required for accurate Triglycerides measurement. Only plain water is allowed.
  • **Avoid:** Alcohol and high-fat meals should be avoided for 24 hours prior.
  • **Timing:** Early morning sample collection is preferred.
