What It Measures

  • **IgE Total:** The primary antibody linked to allergic response.
  • **AEC (Absolute Eosinophil Count):** Immune cells that elevate during allergies.
  • Helps pinpoint environmental or food-based triggers.
  • Useful for persistent sneezing, itching, or asthma symptoms.

Detailed Parameter Breakdown

MarkerTestPurpose
**Allergy Antibody**Immunoglobulin E (IgE) - TotalMeasures general allergic sensitivity

Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** **Not required** for this test.
  • **Medication:** Inform your doctor if you are taking corticosteroids or antihistamines, as they may suppress results.
