What It Measures

  • **Red Blood Cells (RBC):** Oxygen carrying capacity.
  • **White Blood Cells (WBC):** Immune system response to infection.
  • **Platelets:** Blood clotting ability.
  • **Hemoglobin & Hematocrit:** Indicators of Anemia.

Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** **Not required** for this test.
  • **Timing:** Can be collected at any time of the day.
  • **Sample:** Blood sample taken from the arm vein.
