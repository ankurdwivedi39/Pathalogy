Vitamin B12 Test

Measures the level of Vitamin B12 (Cyanocobalamin) in the blood. This vitamin is essential for nerve function, DNA synthesis, and red blood cell formation.

What It Measures

  • **Serum Vitamin B12:** Measures the total amount of B12 available in the bloodstream.
  • Deficiency is linked to anemia, chronic fatigue, and neurological symptoms (numbness/tingling).
  • Essential for individuals on restricted diets, particularly vegetarians and vegans.

Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** Fasting is **recommended (8-10 hours)** for the most accurate baseline reading.
  • **Supplements:** Avoid taking Vitamin B12 supplements for **72 hours (3 days)** prior to the test to prevent falsely high results.
  • **Consultation:** Essential if you experience chronic fatigue or nerve issues.
