Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is mandatory (only water is allowed).
  • **Strenuous Exercise:** **MUST AVOID** intense training, heavy weight lifting, or prolonged endurance exercise for at least 48 hours prior to the test, as this can severely inflate muscle enzyme (CPK/LDH) results.
  • **Hydration:** Ensure excellent hydration by drinking water the night before and morning of the test.
  • **Supplements:** Note all sports supplements (e.g., Creatine, Whey) on the form, as they can affect creatinine and protein results.
