Anyone experiencing fever, chills, body ache, or persistent fatigue, especially during monsoon or flu season. It helps doctors quickly determine the direction of further diagnosis and treatment.

Test Preparation Guidelines (No Fasting Required)

  • **Fasting:** Fasting is **NOT** required for this panel, but please inform the phlebotomist when you last ate.
  • **Hydration:** Ensure good hydration (drink water) before the sample collection.
  • **Timing:** Book the earliest slot possible for urgent results.
  • **Medication:** Inform the phlebotomist of any fever-reducing medication (e.g., paracetamol) taken in the last 12 hours.
