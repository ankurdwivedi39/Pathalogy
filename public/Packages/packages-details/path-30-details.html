Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is usually required for the glucose reading.
  • **Couples:** Both partners should ideally undergo the testing simultaneously for compatibility checks.
  • **Thalassemia:** Avoid blood transfusions for at least three months prior to the Thalassemia screening test.
