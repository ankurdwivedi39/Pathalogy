Test Preparation Guidelines (Medication Timing is Critical)

  • **Fasting:** Fasting is **NOT** required for thyroid hormones.
  • **Thyroid Medication:** If taking L-thyroxine (e.g., Eltroxin, Thyronorm): The sample should be collected **before** taking your morning dose. This is critical for TSH monitoring.
  • **Timing:** Try to take the sample at the same time for all subsequent tests to maintain consistency, as TSH has a circadian rhythm.
  • **Consultation:** Bring your dosage details and a list of all supplements to your follow-up consultation.
