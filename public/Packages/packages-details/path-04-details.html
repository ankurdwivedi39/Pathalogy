Test Preparation Guidelines (Crucial for Accuracy)

  • **Fasting:** Strict 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is mandatory. Only water is allowed.
  • **Medication:** Consult your doctor. For the TSH test, consistency in medication intake timing is crucial.
  • **Avoid:** Avoid alcohol, smoking, or heavy meals 48 hours prior to the test.
  • **Timing:** Recommended sample collection time is early morning (7 AM - 10 AM) for accurate hormone and fasting sugar readings.
