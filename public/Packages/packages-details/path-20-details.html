Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** Mandatory 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is required (only water is allowed).
  • **Caffeine/Sugar:** Avoid coffee, tea, and gum on the morning of the test, as they can affect fasting readings.
  • **Strenuous Exercise:** Avoid intense physical activity 24 hours prior to the test.
  • **Urine Sample:** Please be prepared to provide a urine sample.
