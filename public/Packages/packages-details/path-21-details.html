Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is preferred for Iron Studies and B12 accuracy.
  • **Supplements:** You **MUST** stop taking any oral biotin (Vitamin B7), Vitamin D, or Vitamin B12 supplements for at least 72 hours (3 days) prior to the test to get an accurate baseline reading.
  • **Strenuous Exercise:** Avoid intense physical activity 24 hours prior.
  • **Hydration:** Good hydration is always recommended.
