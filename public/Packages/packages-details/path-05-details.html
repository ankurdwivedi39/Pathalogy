Who Should Book This?

Highly recommended for women aged 40 and above to proactively monitor metabolic changes, check for thyroid imbalances, and screen for early indicators of osteoporosis and cardiovascular risk.

Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is mandatory (only water is allowed).
  • **Thyroid Medication:** If taking medication for the thyroid, please take it as per your usual schedule unless advised otherwise by your doctor. Note the time of the last dose.
  • **Avoid:** Avoid taking calcium or iron supplements 24 hours prior to the test.
  • **Home Collection:** Our female phlebotomists are available on request for home collection.
