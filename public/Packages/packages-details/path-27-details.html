Test Preparation Guidelines (Emphasis on Comfort)

  • **Fasting:** 8-10 hours of overnight fasting is usually required for the glucose reading. Ensure the child understands the fasting requirement.
  • **Gentle Approach:** Our phlebotomists specialize in pediatric collection. Keep the child calm and ready for a quick procedure.
  • **Drinks:** Water is allowed during fasting. Avoid sugary drinks or food.
  • **Supervision:** Parental presence is mandatory during sample collection.
