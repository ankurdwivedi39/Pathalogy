Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** Mandatory 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is highly recommended for accurate enzyme and fat metabolism readings.
  • **Medication:** Do not stop taking any chronic medication. Notify the phlebotomist of any recent over-the-counter pain relievers or supplements, as these can affect liver enzyme levels.
  • **Avoid:** Avoid consuming excessive caffeine or tea on the morning of the test.
  • **Timing:** Early morning samples are ideal.
