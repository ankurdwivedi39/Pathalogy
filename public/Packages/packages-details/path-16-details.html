Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 8-10 hours of overnight fasting is preferred for certain mineral readings (only water is allowed).
  • **Supplements:** Avoid taking Calcium or Vitamin D supplements 24 hours prior to the test for accurate baseline readings.
  • **Strenuous Exercise:** Avoid intense physical activity the day before the test.
  • **Timing:** Early morning sample collection is ideal.
