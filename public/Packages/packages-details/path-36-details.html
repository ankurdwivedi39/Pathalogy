Test Preparation Guidelines (Crucial for Accurate Muscle Markers)

  • **Fasting:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is mandatory (water permitted).
  • **Strenuous Exercise:** You **MUST AVOID ALL VIGOROUS EXERCISE** for at least 48-72 hours prior to collection. Intense exercise severely inflates CPK and LDH levels, invalidating the test's purpose.
  • **Hydration:** Remain well-hydrated throughout the 24 hours leading up to the test.
  • **Supplements:** Note all supplements (Creatine, BCAA, protein powder) on the form.
