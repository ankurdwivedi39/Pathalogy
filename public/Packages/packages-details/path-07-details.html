Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** Mandatory 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is required (only water is allowed).
  • **Medication:** Please make a list of all current medications, including supplements, for the phlebotomist and the reviewing doctor.
  • **Comfort:** Ensure a warm and comfortable environment for the sample collection, as veins can be fragile in senior citizens.
  • **Assistance:** It is recommended to have a family member or caregiver present during the sample collection.
