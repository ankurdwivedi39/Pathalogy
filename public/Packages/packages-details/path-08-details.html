Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is mandatory (only water is allowed).
  • **Exercise:** Avoid strenuous physical activity 24 hours prior to the test to ensure accurate enzyme readings.
  • **Hydration:** Drink plenty of water before the test, as this improves the ease of sample collection.
  • **Sample Time:** Best results are obtained from early morning samples.
