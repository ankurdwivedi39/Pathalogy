Path-17: **Cholesterol Plus**

A vital, focused panel for assessing cardiovascular risk. It measures the complete lipid profile and checks fasting glucose, the two most important indicators for heart disease prevention. [Image of a heart with a circulation diagram]

M.R.P: ₹1100 Final Price: ₹599

Key Benefits & Profiles

  • **Complete Lipid Profile** (8 Tests)
  • **HDL & LDL** ("Good" and "Bad" Cholesterol)
  • **Triglycerides** (Fat levels in blood)
  • **Fasting Glucose** (Immediate Diabetes Screening)
  • Quick Health Snapshot for Annual Check
  • Suitable for all adults over 20 years old

Who Should Book This?

Anyone concerned about high cholesterol, those with a family history of heart disease, or individuals starting a new diet or medication regimen. It is the minimal yearly check recommended for heart health.

Test Preparation Guidelines (Fasting is Critical)

  • **Fasting:** Strict 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is **MANDATORY** for accurate Triglycerides and Glucose readings. Only water is allowed.
  • **Avoid:** No alcohol consumption for 24-48 hours before the test.
  • **Strenuous Exercise:** Avoid intense physical activity the day before the test.
  • **Medication:** Inform the phlebotomist of any cholesterol-lowering medication (statins) being taken.
