Test Preparation Guidelines (Cycle Timing is Critical)

  • **Fasting:** Mandatory 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is required.
  • **Cycle Timing:** Hormonal tests (LH/FSH/E2) are often required to be taken on **Day 2 or Day 3 of the menstrual cycle**. Please consult your gynecologist to confirm the best day for sample collection.
  • **Medication:** Inform your doctor and phlebotomist of any oral contraceptives (OCPs) or hormonal treatment you are currently taking, as they will significantly affect results.
  • **Timing:** Early morning collection is preferred for hormone tests.
