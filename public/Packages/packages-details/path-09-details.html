Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is mandatory for accurate glucose and lipid readings.
  • **PP Sample:** If Post-Prandial (PP) sample is included, you must eat a regular meal and have the blood drawn exactly 2 hours after the first bite.
  • **Medication:** Do not skip your insulin or diabetic medication unless specifically advised by your doctor. Note the time of the last dose.
  • **Logbook:** Bring your blood sugar logbook if you maintain one.
