Test Preparation Guidelines (Morning Timing is Crucial)

  • **Fasting:** Mandatory 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is required.
  • **Timing:** Testosterone levels peak in the early morning. Sample collection **MUST** be done between 7 AM and 10 AM for accurate results.
  • **Strenuous Exercise:** Avoid intense physical activity for 48 hours prior.
  • **Supplements:** Inform the phlebotomist of any hormone-related supplements (e.g., DHEA, Biotin) taken in the last 72 hours.
