Test Preparation Guidelines

  • **Fasting:** Mandatory 10-12 hours of overnight fasting is required (only water is allowed). This is critical for accurate Lipid and Glucose readings.
  • **Medication:** Consult your doctor regarding any medication before the test. Do not stop routine medication unless advised.
  • **Avoid:** Avoid alcohol, tobacco, or strenuous exercise 24 hours prior to sample collection.
  • **Collection:** Ensure a comfortable environment for the phlebotomist during the home collection process.
