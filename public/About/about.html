About Khushipath

Khushipath is a modern pathology system built to simplify diagnostic care. We provide a home sample collection service for patients who prefer comfort and convenience.

For those visiting our center, we offer a smart token system that reduces waiting time and ensures hassle-free experience.

Our mission is to deliver accurate reports, faster services, and a seamless digital experience that makes healthcare more accessible for everyone.
